Girls Workshops

Empowering young women through woodworking education and skill development.

Building Skills, Building Confidence

We believe in empowering young women through hands-on woodworking education. Our workshops provide a supportive, encouraging environment where girls can learn valuable skills, build confidence, and discover the joy of creating with their hands.

Beginner Workshop

Duration: 4 weeks

Age Group: 14-18 years

Skills Covered:

  • Basic tool safety and handling
  • Wood selection and preparation
  • Simple joinery techniques
  • Finishing and sanding
  • Creating a small project

Perfect for those new to woodworking. Learn fundamentals in a supportive environment.

Intermediate Workshop

Duration: 6 weeks

Age Group: 16-22 years

Skills Covered:

  • Advanced joinery techniques
  • Power tool operation
  • Design and planning
  • Furniture construction
  • Professional finishing

Build on your skills with more complex projects and advanced techniques.

Special Projects

Duration: 2-3 weeks

Age Group: All ages

Project Examples:

  • Custom jewelry boxes
  • Decorative wall art
  • Small furniture pieces
  • Wooden accessories
  • Gift items

Short-term workshops focused on specific projects and techniques.
